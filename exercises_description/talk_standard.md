## Talk Standard

### Description
During this exercise, you will talk on a topic based on your interests.

### Example

```
* Free Will vs Determinism: which one is more accurate?
  Source: Philosophy
```

```
* Bringing a fictional character to life: who would you choose and why?
  Source: Books & Literature
```

### AI Interaction

Use `talk_standard` prompt. The prompt instructs the model to generate a topic based on your interests and, later, evaluate your response.

Pipeline:
* Generate a topic (model will do it for you).
* Talk on the topic.
* Evaluate your response using the model.
* Refine the answer based on the model's feedback and your own review.
* Go to the next topic

*Note:* If you don't like the topic, just ask the model to generate a new one.
